About Me

Hello, and thank you for visiting my site. I am a UX and Product Designer. I enjoy designing everything from mobile to web to print. To me, to design is to solve problems. Where I think systematically and consider all the details before making each decision. I believe in research, process, and teamwork. Some of my best works come from collaboration with others.

I am originally from China but grew up in Colorado. There are an excessive amount of outdoor activities in Colorado and I love them all: hiking, rafting, rock climbing, canoeing, snowboarding, skiing, and backpacking. On one of my backpacking trips, we got lost in the mountains for five days and dehydrated for 18 hours.
